Agenda


Hello to all registrants of the 2021 CABW and Cal-SFS Meeting!

The Final Agenda and the Presentation and Speaker Index are both available below.

This past week, you should have all received email reminders from “SWRCB no-reply@zoom.us” with your Zoom Webinar access link. The Bioassessment Program CABW Webpage also contains the link to Register for the Zoom Webinar for those still interested in registering. By registering, you will be able to participate in Q&A during speaker presentations. If you are not registered, you can access the webinar via the live stream on the California Water Quality Monitoring Council YouTube page. You cannot participate in Q&A if you are watching via the live stream. This meeting will be recorded and posted on the Bioassessment Program CABW Webpage for future viewing.

Zoom Webinar protocols for attendees:

  • Chat is disabled

  • Showing your video is disabled

  • Questions can be submitted via the Zoom Webinar Q&A chat feature

  • For assistance on how to use Zoom Webinar and the Q&A chat feature, check out the Zoom - Help Center for Joining and Participating in a Webinar.

  • General questions/concerns please contact SWAMP.

Thank you all for registering and supporting this event! Your attendance and participation, especially in these recent years, has been overwhelmingly appreciated.
